Nycto: Guard Villagers is a small compatibility mod between [Nycto](https://modrinth.com/mod/nycto) and [Guard Villagers](https://modrinth.com/mod/guard-villagers-(fabricquilt))! This mod allows one to turn Villager Guards into thralls under your command! Simply weaken them and then use the Make Thrall power from base Nycto on them.

Vampiric guards will have many of the same traits as their living counterparts such as automatically hunting zombies, while also being able to be set to follow Nycto's thrall commands. They actually make great protectors for your livesto-- I mean villagers.
